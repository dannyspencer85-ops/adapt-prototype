Help & FAQ

How do I change my plan?
Tap any session on Today or Plan to view details. From there, tap Edit. Or talk to the chat: "move Saturday to Sunday."
What does my readiness score mean?
0-100 scale. 85+ is on track. 65-84 means small things to tighten up. Below 65 means real risks. Tap the pill anywhere to see the full breakdown.
What if I'm injured?
Say "my [area] hurts" in chat, or log it at morning check-in. Engine auto-adjusts. See a doctor or PT for actual medical advice — the app flags, it doesn't diagnose.
Can't run today — what do I do?
Tap "Adjust today" on the workout card. Deselect the disciplines you can't do. Workout adapts automatically.
My watch data isn't showing up
This prototype doesn't actually connect to watches yet — data is simulated. In production, you'd link Apple Health or Garmin Connect in Profile → Data sources.
How do notifications work?
When your logged workout data suggests an adjustment, a notification appears on the bell. Tap Accept to apply, Reject to dismiss.
What's Quick Actions vs Coach Chat?
Quick Actions is free and handles plan changes + common training questions with scripted answers. Coach Chat uses Claude AI for open-ended coaching conversations — requires your own Anthropic API key.
I want to start over
Profile tab → "Start over with a new plan." Your current plan resets, you go through onboarding again.
