Notes for review

This page is just a preview — nothing here is in the live app yet. Once you approve, I'll roll the icons + color tokens through the entire app systematically.

  • Color names — Forest, Sand, Bone, Sage, Sky, Amber, Rust, Honey, Pine, Moss. Like them? Want different names?
  • Icon style — outlined, 2px stroke, rounded. Or do you want filled / heavier?
  • Specific icons — any feel wrong? (e.g. is the brick icon clear enough?)
  • Section accent colors — Forest for Today, Rust for Pulse, Sky for Coming up. Mappings okay?
  • Anything else you'd want this design system to cover?
